About Us

Welcome to Raritan's Coffee & Acai, where we bring the rich flavors of authentic Colombian coffee and fresh acai bowls to Raritan, NJ. Our passion for quality beverages and nutritious foods drives everything we do.

We pride ourselves on using only the finest ingredients, from our freshly brewed Colombian coffee to our delicious acai bowls topped with fresh fruits and premium toppings.

Whether you're stopping by for your morning coffee, a nutritious acai bowl, or one of our famous Colombian empanadas, we're dedicated to providing you with an exceptional experience every time you visit.
